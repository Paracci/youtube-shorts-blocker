Blocks Shorts channels, hides Shorts from your feed, and downloads high-quality videos via yt-dlp — entirely on your device.

How it works

• Injects a Block button into every Shorts action bar

• Hides the Shorts shelf via CSS injection on the homepage

• Routes download requests through a native Node.js host running yt-dlp

Privacy

No data is collected or transmitted. All channel blocking and settings are stored locally in your browser via chrome.storage.local.
