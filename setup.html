Installation Instructions

⚠️ Prerequisite: You must have Node.js installed on your computer for the companion app to work.
  1. Open the folder where you unpacked this extension.
  2. Find and double-click the file named install_host.bat.
  3. The script will prompt you for your Extension ID. Click "Copy" below to copy your unique ID, then paste it into the script window (Right-Click to paste in CMD).
    locating...
  4. Press Enter in the script window, and wait for the success message.
  5. Click the "Check Again" button below.

You're all set!

The companion app is connected. You can now download high-quality YouTube videos directly from the player.

Downloader Settings

YouTube frequently updates their systems to block downloads. If your downloads suddenly fail, update the background downloader engine.
